Perfect Installation in 5 Easy Steps

Getting a flawless, bubble-free application is easier than you think! Follow our proven method for professional results every time.

What You'll Need

  • Your Kavacham skin
  • Included installation kit
  • Hair dryer (optional, for curved edges)
  • Well-lit, dust-free environment

Step 1: Prepare Your Device

  1. Power off your device
  2. Remove any existing skins or cases
  3. Clean thoroughly with included alcohol wipe
  4. Wipe with microfiber cloth until spotless
  5. Use dust removal stickers for any particles

Step 2: Align Before Applying

  1. Don't remove backing yet!
  2. Place skin on device to check alignment
  3. Note the key alignment points (camera, buttons, ports)
  4. Practice the placement motion
  5. Visualize where you'll start application

Step 3: Apply the Skin

  1. Peel back only 1-2 inches of backing from top edge
  2. Align perfectly with top of device
  3. Press down the exposed edge firmly
  4. Slowly peel backing while smoothing downward
  5. Use included squeegee to push out air bubbles

Step 4: Perfect the Edges

  1. Work from center outward to edges
  2. For curved edges, apply gentle heat with hair dryer
  3. Press and hold each edge for 10 seconds
  4. Pay special attention to corners
  5. Ensure complete adhesion around ports

Step 5: Final Touches

  1. Run squeegee over entire surface
  2. Check all edges are fully adhered
  3. Remove any remaining tiny bubbles (they'll disappear in 24-48 hours)
  4. Power on device and test all buttons
  5. Enjoy your perfectly protected device!

Pro Tips

  • Temperature matters: Room temperature (70-75°F) is ideal
  • Take your time: Rushing leads to mistakes
  • Start over if needed: The skin can be repositioned within first few minutes
  • Bubble-free guarantee: Our air-channel adhesive eliminates most bubbles automatically

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Applying in dusty environment
  • Not cleaning device thoroughly
  • Removing entire backing at once
  • Stretching the skin during application
  • Applying to wet or cold surfaces
