## 2. FAQ Page (handle: faq)

### Title: Frequently Asked Questions

### Content:

**What makes Kavacham skins different from others?**

Our premium skins feature six key advantages:
- **Accurate Fit**: Precision-cut with 0.1mm tolerance for perfect alignment
- **Zero Residue**: Special adhesive removes cleanly without marks or damage
- **Unlimited Customization**: Design your own or choose from our extensive gallery
- **Bubble-Free**: Air-channel technology eliminates bubbles during application
- **Edge-to-Edge**: Full coverage that works perfectly with cases
- **Anti-Fingerprint**: Special coating resists smudges and oils

**How long do Kavacham skins last?**

With normal use, our premium skins maintain their appearance for 2-3 years. The high-quality vinyl and protective coating ensure colors stay vibrant and edges don't peel.

**Will the skin leave residue when removed?**

Absolutely not! Our advanced adhesive technology ensures zero residue removal. Your device will look exactly as it did before application - guaranteed.

**Is installation really bubble-free?**

Yes! Our air-channel adhesive design allows air to escape during application. Combined with our included installation kit and video guides, you'll achieve a perfect, bubble-free result every time.

**Can I use a case with the skin?**

Definitely! Our skins are precisely cut to ensure compatibility with 99% of cases on the market. The edge-to-edge protection stops exactly where your case begins.

**What devices do you support?**

We currently offer skins for:
- All iPhone models (iPhone 12 and newer)
- Samsung Galaxy S and Note series
- Google Pixel devices
- MacBooks and laptops
- Gaming consoles (PS5, Xbox, Nintendo Switch)
- And we're constantly adding new devices!

**Can I create a custom design?**

Yes! Our design studio lets you upload images, add text, and create completely unique skins. We also offer bulk orders for businesses and teams.

**How do I remove the skin?**

Simply heat the edge with a hair dryer for 15-20 seconds, then slowly peel off. The skin will come off in one piece, leaving zero residue behind.

**Do the skins affect wireless charging?**

Not at all! Our skins are thin enough (0.3mm) to maintain full wireless charging compatibility.

## 8. How to Apply (handle: how-to-apply)

### Title: Installation Guide

### Content:

**Perfect Installation in 5 Easy Steps**

Getting a flawless, bubble-free application is easier than you think! Follow our proven method for professional results every time.

**What You'll Need**

- Your Kavacham skin
- Included installation kit
- Hair dryer (optional, for curved edges)
- Well-lit, dust-free environment

**Step 1: Prepare Your Device**

1. Power off your device
2. Remove any existing skins or cases
3. Clean thoroughly with included alcohol wipe
4. Wipe with microfiber cloth until spotless
5. Use dust removal stickers for any particles

**Step 2: Align Before Applying**

1. **Don't remove backing yet!**
2. Place skin on device to check alignment
3. Note the key alignment points (camera, buttons, ports)
4. Practice the placement motion
5. Visualize where you'll start application

**Step 3: Apply the Skin**

1. Peel back only 1-2 inches of backing from top edge
2. Align perfectly with top of device
3. Press down the exposed edge firmly
4. Slowly peel backing while smoothing downward
5. Use included squeegee to push out air bubbles

**Step 4: Perfect the Edges**

1. Work from center outward to edges
2. For curved edges, apply gentle heat with hair dryer
3. Press and hold each edge for 10 seconds
4. Pay special attention to corners
5. Ensure complete adhesion around ports

**Step 5: Final Touches**

1. Run squeegee over entire surface
2. Check all edges are fully adhered
3. Remove any remaining tiny bubbles (they'll disappear in 24-48 hours)
4. Power on device and test all buttons
5. Enjoy your perfectly protected device!

**Pro Tips**

- **Temperature matters**: Room temperature (70-75°F) is ideal
- **Take your time**: Rushing leads to mistakes
- **Start over if needed**: The skin can be repositioned within first few minutes
- **Bubble-free guarantee**: Our air-channel adhesive eliminates most bubbles automatically

**Common Mistakes to Avoid**

- Applying in dusty environment
- Not cleaning device thoroughly
- Removing entire backing at once
- Stretching the skin during application
- Applying to wet or cold surfaces

**Video Tutorial**

Scan the QR code on your installation kit for our step-by-step video guide, available in multiple languages.

**Need Help?**

If you encounter any issues during installation, email support@kavacham.com with photos, and we'll guide you through or send a replacement if needed.
